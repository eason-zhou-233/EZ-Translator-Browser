# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

EZ Translator ("the Extension") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personally identifiable information.

The Extension does not require account registration.

The Extension does not track browsing history beyond the functionality required to perform translation operations initiated by the user.

## How Translation Works

When the user requests translation, the selected text or webpage content may be sent to a translation service chosen by the user, including:

* Google Translate
* User-configured OpenAI-compatible API providers

The transmitted content is used solely for generating translation results.

## Local Storage

The Extension may store the following information locally within the browser:

* Translation settings
* User-configured API endpoints
* User-configured model parameters
* Translation cache

This data remains on the user's device and is not transmitted to the developer.

## Third-Party Services

Translation requests may be sent directly from the user's browser to third-party services configured by the user.

The developer of EZ Translator does not control, access, or store any data processed by these third-party services.

Users should review the privacy policies of the translation providers they choose to use.

## Data Sharing

The Extension does not sell, rent, transfer, or share user data with any third parties.

## Security

The Extension processes translation requests locally within the browser and communicates directly with user-selected translation services.

No user data is intentionally collected by the developer.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any updates will be published on this page.

## Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the project maintainer through the GitHub repository.
